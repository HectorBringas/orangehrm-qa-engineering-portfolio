# Scope

## Objective

The objective of this project is to identify the business areas with the highest impact within OrangeHRM and prioritize testing efforts using a risk-based approach. The scope focuses on modules that directly affect security, employee management, system access, and core business operations.

## In Scope

- Login & Authentication
- Admin (Roles & Permissions)
- PIM (Employee Management)
- Recruitment
- Leave
- My Info
- Maintenance (subject to future review)

## Out of Scope

- Dashboard
- Directory
- Claim
- Time
- Performance
- Buzz

The modules excluded from the project scope were considered lower priority for this portfolio initiative. While they provide business value, their unavailability is less likely to impact business continuity, security, data integrity, or the critical business processes identified during the risk assessment. In most cases, temporary workarounds or manual procedures could be applied while issues are being resolved.

## Assumptions and Constraints

- The project will use the public OrangeHRM demo environment.
- No access to the application source code is available.
- No direct access to the production database is available.
- Some modules or features may be limited by the demo environment.
- Due to project time constraints, testing activities will focus on the areas identified as highest priority during the risk assessment.
